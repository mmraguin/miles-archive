# Goals — 2026

> *Current year goals. Updated from Notion export, March 2026.*

## What Matters To Me

**Comfort** — When I choose comfort, I think clearer, relate better, and actually enjoy being here. It keeps me honest about what I can do.

**Support** — Support reminds me I don’t have to carry everything alone. It tells me my needs are valid and heard.

**Growth** — Growth keeps me moving instead of staying stuck in habits that don’t help me anymore.


## Who I Want To Be

**Independent** — I rely on myself in practical and emotional ways. I want to make decisions that fit me, manage my life with clarity, and not feel controlled by fear, pressure, or other people’s expectations. I want support to be a bonus, not a crutch.

**Present** — I actually be in my life while it’s happening. I don’t want to run on autopilot or constantly be in my head. I want to notice what I feel, what my body says, what I enjoy, and who I’m with. I want to experience my days instead of racing through them.

**Healthy** — I take care of my body and mind with honesty and respect. I want habits that support me long-term, not quick fixes or punishment. I want to listen to my limits, improve slowly, and build a lifestyle that supports me feeling well more often than not.
