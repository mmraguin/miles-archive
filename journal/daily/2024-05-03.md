## Journal
Just because your mental health isn’t in great shape doesn’t mean you have a condition. And just because you live with a mental condition doesn’t mean your mental health is always in a bad place.

Everyone has unresolved issues, but not all have trauma. Most of us are egotistic, but not all are a narcissist. We worry, stress, overthink, and freak out, but not all have anxiety. We feel deeply sad, but not all battle depression. Most ponder death, but not all wish for it. Everyone has mood swings, struggles with focus, consumes in excess, or has a complex relationship with their diets… but not all are bipolar, neurodivergent, or addicted.

Language matters. Education is important. Understanding is important. Mindset is important. Self-awareness is important. Most of all, access to help and support is crucial.
