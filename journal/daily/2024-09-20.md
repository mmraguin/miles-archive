# Daily Journal — Friday, 2024-09-20

## Journal
I’m on the verge of a panic attack, and I think it’s because I know that tomorrow is the day I willingly walk into something that is a source of trauma and strong anxiety, and I have no say in it.

The thought in itself is nothing big: it will be in the office, I will be seated, there’s a lactation room, there’s nothing I’m uncertain of, and I know the people there. It also helps that I know people will be there earlier than I will, so I don’t feel like I will be cheated.

There’s something I can’t figure out though: am I anxious because it’s work, because it’s tribal, or because it’s a social event? What am I fearing? The thought that I’m unsafe?

I did have a strong memory about feeling largely unsafe from Bangkok, and Sam being my safety blanket.

I just wish that people knew the extent to which anxiety is crippling and that it’s not “just in the mind,” nor is it “a matter of willpower” or powering through.
